Accessibility Tools for Disabled Persons

  • Sign Language Forum - A forum for American Sign Language (ASL) discussion and learning.
  • ASL Dictionary - An online American Sign Language dictionary for looking up signs.
  • Signing Savvy - A sign language dictionary containing several thousand high-resolution videos of American Sign Language (ASL) signs.
  • Tools of the Blind and Visually Impaired - To support children and adults that are blind or visually impaired due to CRB1 LCA/RP through funding research to treat this
    rare eye disease as well as connect those affected to resources for succeeding without sight.
    We are parents, patients and professionals working together to achieve these goals.
  • NVDA - NonVisual Desktop Access (NVDA) is a free and open-source screen reader for the Windows operating system.
  • JAWS Screen Reader - Job Access With Speech (JAWS) is a popular screen reader for individuals with visual impairments.
  • Zoom - A video conferencing tool with accessibility features such as closed captioning and screen reader support.
  • Apple Accessibility - Information about accessibility features built into Apple products, including VoiceOver for the blind and low vision.
  • Google TalkBack - TalkBack is a screen reader for Android devices developed by Google.
