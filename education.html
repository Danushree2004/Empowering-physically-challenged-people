Education Resources for Disabled Students in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu Open University

Tamil Nadu Open University (TNOU) provides distance education programs accessible to disabled students.
Visit their website for more information: http://www.tnou.ac.in/

Disability Rights Alliance Tamil Nadu (DRATN)

DRATN is an organization advocating for the rights of disabled persons in Tamil Nadu. They offer resources and support for disabled students.
Visit their website: https://dratn.org/

Tamil Nadu Textbook Corporation

Tamil Nadu Textbook Corporation provides accessible textbooks and learning materials for disabled students.
Contact them for assistance: http://www.textbookcorp.tn.gov.in/

Tamil Nadu Directorate of Technical Education

The Directorate of Technical Education in Tamil Nadu offers inclusive education programs for disabled students in technical fields.
Explore their initiatives: https://www.tndte.gov.in/

Resource Centre for the Visually Impaired (RCVI)

RCVI in Tamil Nadu provides support and resources for visually impaired students.
Learn more about their services: https://rcvi.ac.in/
